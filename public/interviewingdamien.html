Interviewing Damien

Despite your desire to speak to either Brendan or Francesca about Alyssa's affair, you next decide to interview Damien, Carolyn's husband. It turns out that Damien worked with Alyssa at the same finance company. Adding to the intrigue you have already discovered, Damien informs you that his wife Carolyn had been having an affair with Alyssa's husband Brendan for several months. He says that Alyssa knew all about it, although she hadn't confronted either her husband or her best friend about the affair yet. In fact, he and Alyssa had had a conversation about it only the evening before. He had bumped into her as she was coming out of the CCTV control room, where the CCTV cameras for the chalet village were monitored. Come to think of it, he wasn't sure what she could have been doing in there, and she had been acting rather strangely. Maybe there would be something interesting to find in there? Sadly he doesn't know the code to the door lock though, so once again you'll need to puzzle it out for yourself.
