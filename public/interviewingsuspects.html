Interviewing the suspects

First to be interviewed is Greta, the chalet maid. Unfortunately, just as you greet her she starts having an asthma attack and needs her inhaler, which is locked inside her cleaning case. She can't speak to tell you the code to unlock it, so you need to work it out. She gestures vaguely towards her notebook, and as you pick it a up a number of polaroid photographs tumble out, each with a handwritten note on it.
