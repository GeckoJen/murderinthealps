Interviewing Edmund

Fifth to be interviewed is Edmund. He admits that he was having an affair with Alyssa. He says it started after Alyssa discovered the affair between Brendan and Carolyn. He was providing comfort and one thing led to another. He says that he and Alyssa used to send each other coded messages so that neither her husband nor his wife would be able to read them if they happened to intercept them. You show him the coded note that Alyssa was holding when she died. Edmund looks astonished. He confirms that it appears to be written in their secret code, but adamantly denies that he sent it to her. There was no need to send notes, he explained, when they were staying in the same chalet. He says somebody must have cracked their code and sent a note to Alyssa pretending that it was from him. He tells you that if you can access Alyssa's laptop, which is lying on the coffee table in the chalet, he can give you the key to decrypt the message. You set to work trying to work out her password.
