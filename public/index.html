Murder in the Alps

Virtual Escape Room from Gecko Escape Rooms

Murder in the Alps is a virtual escape room designed to test your crime solving abilities. A group of 6 friends were holidaying together at a ski resort, but late one night, one of them is found murdered. You are a detective with the local police force, and you need to interview all the suspects, and carefully sift through the information they provide to figure out whodunnit and whytheydunnit!

A story full of intrigue and betrayal, it's up to you to seek justice for the murder victim and build a watertight case to ensure the murderer can't get away with it. Do you have what it takes to solve the mystery?
