At the murder scene

Arriving at the chalet you first go to the sauna to view the body and the scene of the murder. Alyssa has been killed by several blows to the head with a blunt object - the blood-spattered snow shovel next to her body seems the likely murder weapon. Her head is lying in a small pool of blood, and there is a trail of bloody footprints running away from the body. You measure the footprints to establish shoe size. Alyssa is still clutching in her hand a piece of paper with a message written on it. It must have been written in code, however, as it makes no sense to you.
