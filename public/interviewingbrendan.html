Interviewing Brendan

Second to be interviewed is Brendan, the victim's husband. He appears to be utterly distraught and claims he doesn't know why anybody could possibly want to hurt Alyssa. You're not sure you believe him, but you move on for now. You ask him about his shoes and he looks at you blankly. He says all the shoes for everyone in the chalet are kept in a storage locker by the front door, next to where they keep all the skis. There's a code to unlock it but he doesn't seem to be in a fit state to tell you what it is. You'll have to figure it out for yourself.
